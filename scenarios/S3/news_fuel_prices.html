Petrol Prices Surge to PKR 293 per Litre

Date: May 15, 2026

ISLAMABAD: The Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA) has announced an immediate hike in petrol and diesel prices by PKR 15 per litre. The new price of petrol stands at PKR 293. This is expected to significantly impact transportation and logistics costs across the country, especially for inter-city freight from Karachi to Lahore.
