Government raises petrol price by Rs 8 per litre

ISLAMABAD — The government raised petrol prices by Rs 8 per litre, taking the rate to Rs 274.17 per litre effective midnight Wednesday.

High Speed Diesel prices were also raised by Rs 6.12 per litre to Rs 268.44.

Industry experts warn that the fuel price increase will raise logistics costs by approximately 12 to 15 percent across Pakistan's supply chain, particularly affecting last-mile delivery operations in Punjab.

Electronics wholesalers in Lahore report that transport costs for large appliances have increased by PKR 800 to 1200 per unit depending on distance from warehouse to delivery point.
